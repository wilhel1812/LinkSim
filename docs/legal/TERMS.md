# Terms and Acceptable Use

Last updated: 2026-03-13

By using LinkSim, you agree to use it responsibly and lawfully.

## Acceptable use

You must not:
- misuse the service for abuse, spam, or harassment
- attempt unauthorized access or privilege escalation
- automate excessive requests against upstream data providers
- upload or publish content you do not have rights to use

## Accounts and access

- Access may be approval-gated.
- Roles define what you can view/edit/manage.
- Admins/moderators may suspend access for policy violations.

## User content

- You remain responsible for content you create or upload.
- You must respect third-party data and map/source licenses.
- You must not store sensitive secrets in app content (passwords, private keys, access tokens, or similar credentials).
- Resource visibility/access levels are intended for collaboration and clutter control, not confidential secret storage.

## Service operation

- The service may change, be limited, or be unavailable without notice.
- Access can be restricted to protect system integrity or comply with policy/legal requirements.
